Department of Truth

You are a government office of the Department of Truth (you know what it is if you've read 1984), and your job is to control the public opinion to protect the government.

In January, 2022, a severe virus broke out in the country, your job is to monitor the panic of the society and control it by controlling the data related to the desease, as high level panic in the society will cause instability and threat the power of your government

you will lose the game if the public panic index is too high (above 10, you win if you manage the index well till April, when the situation gets better)

To control the data, you have a bunch of tools listed in the toolbox, each consumes different conputational power and have different effectives on the data, there are descriptions but you also need to use your common sense to understand the function and the case. But be careful, abuse of such power will cause the public to untrust the governemnt, you will also lose the game if the Government Untrust Index reaches 10
